Down to the Minute

Years of minute data for over 1,000 US Equities are available in your playground. Put your ideas to the test through storm and snow, wind and rain. How will you overcome?

Explore What's Out There

We've collected and compiled many algorithms for you to explore. With just a click you can test beginner's strategies to advanced Machine Learning classifiers. Investigate what works best and why. Batteries are all included.

Visualize the Future

Raise the bar with beautiful charts. Derive sharp insights on correlation, trends, and performance. Compare your results to the benchmark's performance. Learn how to maximize your alpha.
